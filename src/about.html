Ouvrages Collection

The Project

This collaboration is an experiment and collective adventure to celebrate wine, nature and art.

The Vineyard

We are in the heart of Burgundy, in Cote d'or between Beaune and Nuits-Saint-Georges.

Our work & philosophy

Angèle cultivates her two plots with her horse. All the work is done by hand. Once the grapes have been harvested, the wine-making process is as natural and respectful as possible. No input or little sulphites are used in order to keep a maximum of fruit and autenthicity. Giving love to the living and letting the power of intention guide us is the motto of viticulture in its production path.

The Creators

Angèle and M share a common vision of promoting viticulture and art. This partnership brings together multiple perspectives around creating, collecting, and building a community into one cohesive project.

Pairing

Each bottle produced is adorned with art made in collaboration with the artist for the collection. We carefully consider the association between each piece of art and the wine to ensure that the emotions evoked by drinking or viewing great art are intrinsically linked.

The Collectors

We want collectors to be part of a collective adventure and community to celebrate art and natural viticulture.

How does Ouvrages Work ?

When you buy an NFT artwork you can get a bottle of our limited vintage. The NFT is printed on the label of the unique bottle.

How many Art NFTs and bottles are there ?

Each artist works on a mini-collection.

What about shipping ?

We offer worldwide delivery. Depending on your location and local shipping rules it could take up to few weeks to arrive following the purchase of your NFT. We will will strive to get it to you as quickly as possible. See our shipping page for more information.
